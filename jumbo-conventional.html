Jumbo Loans

Jumbo loans finance properties that exceed the conforming loan limits set by Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac — currently $766,550 in most U.S. markets, higher in designated high-cost areas. Sally specializes in navigating the unique underwriting requirements of jumbo financing, including stricter credit thresholds, larger down payment structures, and detailed asset verification.

  • ✦ Loan amounts from $766,551 up to $3M+
  • ✦ Fixed and adjustable rate options
  • ✦ Primary residences, second homes & investment
  • ✦ Competitive rates through elite lender network
  • ✦ Available in CA, TX, CO & NV

02

Conventional Loans

Conventional loans are the gold standard for buyers with solid credit and standard documentation. Not government-backed, these loans offer broad flexibility in property types and loan structures. With down payments as low as 3%, conventional financing is accessible for a wide range of qualified buyers seeking competitive rates and lower overall costs.

  • ✦ Loan amounts up to conforming limits
  • ✦ Down payments as low as 3%
  • ✦ Fixed 10, 15, 20, or 30-year terms
  • ✦ PMI removable at 20% equity
  • ✦ Single-family, condo & multi-unit eligible
