Zero Down Loans

A zero down mortgage allows qualified buyers to purchase a home without any upfront down payment. Sally works with USDA Rural Development loans, VA loans, select state bond programs, and lender-specific zero-down products to find the right fit. These programs are ideal for buyers who have strong income and credit but prefer to preserve cash reserves for moving costs, renovations, or emergencies.

  • ✦ No down payment required for qualified buyers
  • ✦ USDA, VA, and lender-specific zero-down options
  • ✦ Keep your savings intact at closing
  • ✦ Competitive fixed interest rates available
  • ✦ Available in CA, TX, CO & NV

02

FHA Loans

FHA loans are insured by the Federal Housing Administration and are designed specifically to help buyers with lower credit scores or limited savings achieve homeownership. With a minimum down payment of just 3.5% for buyers with a credit score of 580 or higher, FHA financing opens the door for millions of Americans who would otherwise be locked out of the market. Sally has guided countless first-time buyers through the FHA process with clarity and confidence.

  • ✦ Down payments as low as 3.5%
  • ✦ Credit scores as low as 580 considered
  • ✦ Higher debt-to-income ratios accepted
  • ✦ Gift funds allowed for down payment
  • ✦ Single-family homes, condos & 2–4 unit properties
INQUIRE ABOUT FHA
03

VA Loans

VA loans are one of the most powerful mortgage benefits available to U.S. military veterans, active-duty service members, and eligible surviving spouses. Backed by the Department of Veterans Affairs, VA loans offer zero down payment, no private mortgage insurance (PMI), and highly competitive interest rates. Sally is deeply committed to honoring the service of veterans by helping them navigate every step of the VA loan process with expert guidance.

  • ✦ Zero down payment — no PMI required
  • ✦ Available to veterans, active duty & surviving spouses
  • ✦ Competitive rates — often lower than conventional
  • ✦ Limited closing costs by VA guidelines
  • ✦ Can be used multiple times throughout your lifetime

WHO QUALIFIES?

Find Out If You're Eligible

Sally personally evaluates each client's financial profile to determine which program delivers the strongest outcome. Here's a quick overview of who typically qualifies for each program.

Zero Down

  • Stable employment & income history
  • Good to excellent credit (620+)
  • Property in eligible rural or suburban area (USDA)
  • Meet income limits for program area
  • Primary residence purchase only

FHA Loans

  • Credit score 580+ (3.5% down)
  • Credit score 500–579 (10% down)
  • Steady 2-year employment history
  • Debt-to-income ratio generally under 57%
  • Primary residence only; property must meet FHA standards

VA Loans

  • Active duty, veteran, or eligible surviving spouse
  • Minimum 90 days active duty (wartime) or 181 days (peacetime)
  • National Guard & Reserve: 6 years of service
  • Valid Certificate of Eligibility (COE)
  • Satisfactory credit & sufficient income
